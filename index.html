Chenyang Wang

Lecturer in Econometrics

Current teaching

Postgraduate modules in Introduction to Econometrics and Further Econometrics.

Biography

I studied my PhD degree in Economics at the University of Birmingham (Birmingham, the UK, Supervisor: Prof Alessandra Guariglia). After completing my PhD in 2021 I worked as a Lecturer at University of Reading before starting as a teaching associate at the University of Manchester in 2022 Dec. I became a lecturer in Econometrics at the UoM since 2024 Sep. I mainly focus on teaching econometrics for the postgraduate students. I love to teach quantiative modules (statistics and econometrics) and use a plain language to demonstrate complex concepts.

Resarch interests

My research mainly focuses on the following areas:

*How liquidity constraints affect entrepreneurship in China and the UK

*The role of investor sentiments played in Chinese stock market

*How to help UG/PG students to better form up into a hertergenous nationalities study group (Scholarship)

*How to help UG/PG students to better select the optional modules (Scholarship)
